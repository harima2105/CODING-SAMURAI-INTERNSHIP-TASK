# CODING-SAMURAI-INTERNSHIP-TASK
This repository contains Excel-based data analysis dashboards and reports created during my Business Analytics Internship at Coding Samurai.
The projects focus on analyzing business data, visualizing trends, and generating insights to support decision-making.

**1️. Sales Metrics Dashboard**

**Objective:** Track key sales performance indicators.

**Highlights:**

Total revenue, quantity sold, and average price KPIs

Top-selling products and regions

Interactive filters for dynamic insights

**2. Employee Demographic Analysis**

**Objective:** Understand the demographic distribution of employees.

**Highlights:**

Department-wise employee distribution

Gender and age visualizations.

Average salary by department

**3️. Monthly Sales Trends and Analysis**

**Objective:** Identify sales trends over time.

**Highlights:**

Monthly sales totals shown in a line chart

Best and worst month identification

Region-wise heatmap to compare performanceIdentify sales trends over time.
